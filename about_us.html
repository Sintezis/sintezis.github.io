We are ...

software development company based in Zagreb with top developers and designers from Europe and United States that cover major technologies and have years of experience in building digital products ranging from web and desktop SaaS to games and mobile apps.

We are a team of practical problem solvers who utilize a broad set of skills in their product development process. We distinguish ourselves by approaching our projects through detailed product life cycle management, rather than focusing on only the service or application platform, we carefully tend to each phase of product life cycle. With more than 10 years of experience we have seen many products fail, and have built our team around our solutions for common pitfalls when developing new products. Among our services we offer full stack web development, SaaS development, product design and development, and game development and design.
