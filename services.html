Idea To MVP

Merges our product development and software development protocol into a single time constrained, fixed price process. Ideal for early stage funded startups that lack development team and technical directorship. While a full working MVP can be produced in a short time, it worth to mention that a minimum of 3 months is needed to produce a good MVP. In contrast the MVP phase shouldn't last longer than 6 months, in which case it indicates poor design/communication, bad idea. Precisely why we encourage startups to go through Product development first.
